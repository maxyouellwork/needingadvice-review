User Experience Issues

Critical: Mobile Usability

On mobile devices (60%+ of mortgage-related searches), a persistent popup widget with the text "Would you like us to call you?" covers the main content area, making articles unreadable without dismissing it.

Additionally, a sticky footer bar containing contact options consumes approximately 15% of the mobile viewport. Combined with the popup, this leaves limited space for actual content consumption.

Likely impact: Elevated bounce rates and reduced time on site for mobile visitors.

Design Dated

The visual design reflects web standards from approximately 2015. While functional, it may not inspire the same level of trust as modernised competitor sites. Elements such as cartoon character illustrations appear dated compared to current design trends in the financial services sector.

Competing Calls-to-Action

Multiple contact mechanisms compete for attention simultaneously: sticky footer bar, sidebar contact form, popup widget, and in-content phone numbers. Research indicates that excessive options can create decision paralysis and reduce conversion rates.
